Grim

 

 

 
















Rules

You have died and now you must play the Grim for your chance to live again; he loves Yahtzee . The rules are that of your typical every day Yahtzee with just a one exception: The Grim does not allow more than one Yahtzee (humans don't deserve to get lucky more than once).

Non-automatic Scores (you must choose)

Ones: Roll as many ones as you can
Twos: Roll as many twos as you can
Threes: Roll as many threes as you can
Fours: Roll as many fours as you can
Fives: Roll as many fives as you can
Sixes: Roll as many sixes as you can
Three of a Kind: Roll three of the same number and recieve the sum of all dice in points
Four of a Kind: Roll four of the same number and recive the sum of all dice in points
Full House: Roll three of a kind and a pair, recieve 25 points
Small Straight: Roll four sequential numbers and recieve 30 points
Large Straight: Roll five sequential numbers and recieve 40 points
Chance Dump your numbers if you have nothing else and recive the sum of all dice in points
Yahtzee: Roll five of a kind and recive 50 points; no multiple yahtzees allowed

Automatic Scores

Bonus: Recieve 35 points if your ones, twos,..., etc. totalled equals 63 points or higher
Total: The totalled final score that decides your fate

Extra

Click on the ghost at anytime to change accessories
Click "roll" to roll new values and end your turn if all dice are selected
Click on a die to hold that value and click it again to let go of it
